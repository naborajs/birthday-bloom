# SEO Optimization Guide 🔎

## 🔍 Search Engine Visibility
Birthday Bloom is built with SEO best practices to ensure your surprise link looks professional and ranks well.

## 🏷️ The SEO Metadata Stack

### 1. Open Graph (Social Sharing)
When you share your link on WhatsApp, Discord, or X (Twitter), it will show:
- **Title**: Dynamic based on `VITE_BIRTHDAY_NAME`.
- **Image**: High-fidelity OG image located in `public/`.
- **Site Name**: **Birthday Bloom by Nishant Sarkar**.
- **Achievement**: Ensuring 100% click-through rate optimization.

### 2. Search Engine Indexing
- **Sitemap**: A pre-generated `sitemap.xml` ensures Google Bots crawl the celebration logic.
- **Robots.txt**: Explicitly allows all crawlers while blocking private `/tmp/` or node paths.

### 3. Schema.org (JSON-LD)
We use a `CreativeWork` schema to tell search engines that this site is a personalized digital product. This improves the "Rich Snippet" appearance in Google Search results.
